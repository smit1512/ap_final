HISTORY

The foundation of AP Autolink Pvt. Ltd. was laid 36 Years ago by Mr. Shailesh Shah with a small unit in Ahmedabad.He has established many brands in Trading & Manufacturing.It started with the vision of providing durable products without compromising on quality.

ACHIEVEMENTS

Today, AP Autolink Pvt. Ltd. is considered as the fastest growing company in Gujarat,India.Within a short time span, we have established a strong presence – in both Domestic & Global Markets.Our strategy is to offer a robust product portfolio across categories.Our focussed Approach, Aggressive Pricing Strategies and Superior quality have made us stand apart from the competition in the market.

FACILITIES

Over the years, AP Autolink Pvt. Ltd. continues to invest in state-of-the-art facilities harbouring world-class capacities and capabilities. Our facilities include fully automated forging and machining lines, among the best in the industry. Today, AP Autolink Pvt. Ltd. holds the capability to meet the global demands of its customers with seamless engineering and design support.

QUALITY SYSTEM

At AP Autolink Pvt. Ltd. quality and customer focus is of utmost importance. We strongly believe in providing total customer satisfaction through excellence in product & process development, building quality into the process, proactive approach to quality issues, quick response to customer, cutting-edge technology & price competitiveness.

About Us

AP Autolink Pvt. Ltd. was established in 1994 and has become a leading provider of automotive parts.AP has become fastest growing autoparts company in Gujarat,India.We are known for providing durable products without compromising on quality.We ventured into the production of plastic injection molded ,sheet metal molded, painted components for the automobiles engineering sector since 1994.AP manufactures visors, fender, tail panels, side panels, visor glass ,fuel tank (petrol tank), silencer for all types of motorcycles.
